A systematic and thorough method for identifying the significant links in biochemical pathways with high sensitivity.

Roadmap

FEvER analysis and software is not necessarily a polished product but rather a proof of concept. While the software is ready to use as it is, there might be small bugs in the code, and the project will hopefully continue to develop in the future. A brief description of the roadmap for development in the foreseable future will be posted here.

Known Issues

Below are the known issues associated with the FEvER software:

  • A minor bug occasionally causes a glitch in run-animation. This glitch has no implications on the test outcome.
